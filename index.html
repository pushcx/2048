Note: The game on this site is the original version of 2048. Apps for iOS and Android are also available.

Eleven is a variant of 2048. Numbers are replaced with their log2, colors are improved, and tiles animate faster after a while. Scoring is unchanged, so you can fairly copare scores between this and the original 2048. Thank you to Gabriele Cirulli for allowing people to make derivatives.

Created by Gabriele Cirulli. Based on 1024 by Veewo Studio and conceptually similar to Threes by Asher Vollmer. Inartfully hacked on by Peter Harkins to display the log2 of tile value.
